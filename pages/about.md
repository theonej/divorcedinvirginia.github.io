# About

*Divorce in Virginia* is a practical resource designed to help individuals navigate one of life's most challenging transitions — a process that is often emotionally overwhelming, legally complex, and deeply personal.

This guide was created by someone who has walked this path firsthand, and understands the weight of what you may be facing. Whether your situation is straightforward or high-conflict, the goal here is to provide honest, grounded guidance to help you move forward with clarity, protect what matters most, and emerge with as little lasting harm as possible — to yourself, and to everyone involved.
